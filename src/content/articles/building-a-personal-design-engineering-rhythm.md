Creative work breaks when every week is pure execution. Product work breaks when every week is pure exploration.

The most reliable balance is to time-box both.

## A practical weekly loop
1. Monday: define one design risk and one engineering risk.
2. Tuesday and Wednesday: ship the highest-confidence path.
3. Thursday: harden edge cases and production behavior.
4. Friday: document decisions and archive learnings.

This rhythm keeps momentum while preserving enough space for experimentation.

## Why it works
The loop creates clear transitions between ideation, implementation, and stabilization. Teams stop treating quality as a last-mile activity.
